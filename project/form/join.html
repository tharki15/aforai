About Bajrang Dal

Bajrang Dal is the youth wing of Vishva Hindu Parishad (VHP), dedicated to the service of Hindu society and protection of Hindu values.

The organization was founded on 1 October 1984 in Uttar Pradesh, India. The name "Bajrang" is a reference to the Hindu deity Hanuman.

Objectives:

  • To organize Hindu youth for nation-building activities
  • To work for the protection of Hindu society and culture
  • To promote social harmony and national integrity
  • To provide relief during natural calamities

Why Join Bajrang Dal?

By joining Bajrang Dal, you become part of a national movement working for:

  • Protection of Hindu Dharma and culture
  • Service to society through various programs
  • Character building and physical fitness
  • National security awareness
  • Disaster relief and social service activities

Members get opportunities for leadership development, self-defense training, and participation in nation-building activities.
